Everything you need

One file. Every feature.

No app store. No subscription. No server. Just download, open in your browser, and start studying.

Lecture Tracker
Organise every lecture inside its chapter. Track planned vs watched time, pace projection, and per-subject due dates.
Live Session Timer
Drift-free timer with pause/resume. Pomodoro mode built in. One tap to start — lecture, reading, revision, questions, or test.
Visual Timeline
Day × hour grid showing every session as a coloured bar. Click any bar for details, edit, or delete. Adjustable daily target.
Syllabus Mastery
Per-chapter mastery table with confidence stars, revision dots (R1–R5), first-read date, reading time, and inline notes.
Spaced Revisions
Two-layer system: daily "review yesterday" nudge + chapter spaced R1–R5 queue (1/7/30/60/90 days). Never miss a revision.
Tests with Countdown
Real exam simulation — reading phase then writing phase countdown. Complete or Abort. Results tracked with score history.
Flashpoints
Capture key formulas, traps, and concepts during study. Chapter-grouped, starred, pinned, and exportable to CSV.

Dashboard
Study heatmap, line chart, subject progress, weak chapters, streak, avg test score, and exam countdown — all in one view.
Final Revision Planner
Per-paper passes (e.g. 7/3/1 days), anchored backward from exam date. Reorder papers per pass. Launch revision sessions directly.
Cloud Sync via Gist
Bring your own GitHub token. Zero infrastructure on our side. Auto-pull on startup, merge-by-id, dirty-flag requeue. Works offline first.
